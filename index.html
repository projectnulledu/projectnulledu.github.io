About Project Null Education

Project Null Education is a company that provides coding lessons both in person and online. Our mission is to empower people to learn to code and become confident developers.

Our lessons are taught by experienced developers who are passionate about teaching and helping others learn. We offer a range of courses for all skill levels, from beginners to advanced developers.

Our courses cover a variety of programming languages and frameworks, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, and more.

To enroll in our courses, you must be willing to put in the time and effort to learn.




Pricing

We offer three pricing tiers:

  • Basic: $15 per month (4 Sessions)
  • Pro: $30 per month (8 Sessions)
  • Ultimate: $60 per month (16 Sessions)

All pricing tiers include access to our online courses. Pro and Ultimate tiers include additional benefits such as one-on-one coaching and access to more exclusive content.

All prices are in Canadian Dollars (CAD) and each session is approx. 20-30 minutes long
